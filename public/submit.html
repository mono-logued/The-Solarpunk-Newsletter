How to submit media to The Solarpunk Newsletter

Accepted forms of media

Submissions should either be in the solarpunk genre, or relate to turning our world solarpunk. We accept any form of media that we can publish on our site, including:

  • Text (fiction, essays, poetry, letters, etc.),
  • graphic art
  • video (essays, fiction), and
  • audio (podcasts, music).

Non-textual submissions should be accompanied with a text description or introduction to the main media. You may optionally submit an author bio along with the media.

Submission process

Send your submission, or a link to it, to solarpunknewsletter@gmail.com. You may submit your own work, or that of another if it is suitably licensed.

The editors will decide whether we want to include your media in the newsletter. If we decide to include it, we may do some editing for length or minor editing to fit the format of the newsletter. Any editing will aim to preserve the spirit of the original, and note the editing (as required by the license).

Solarpunks believe in everyone living well without degrading the environment. There's no place for hate in this vision, so we will not accept any hate speech.

Licensing

Part of the purpose of the newsletter is to build a media commons for our solarpunk society. All media submitted to the newsletter should be under a creative commons license that allows us to edit and republish it under a similar license. We prefer CC-BY-SA, but will also accept CC-BY, and will share our derivatives as CC-BY-SA. We do not accept the No Derivatives form of the license because we may need to edit the content to fit within the newsletter and because we encourage people to republish our newsletter other forms which may require further editing. We no not accept the Non-Commercial form of the license as offline remixes may require sales to cover production costs.
